ABOUT ME

I am a COMPUTER SCIENCE Graduate. My teenage years were spent wondering about how ideas like online shopping, food delivery, and taxi booking became reality. I was fascinated when I received an email from Amazon for each order and when Swiggy showed the estimated delivery time for my food. All those application features, which I now know are API integrations, were a big deal for me back then. Not just these, I also tried to fathom how my phone took videos in slow motion, and now I am figuring out how Siri works and how Netflix gives me just the right recommendations. From watching Tom & Jerry DVDs on television to binge-watching series on Amazon Prime, I believe our generation has witnessed significant technological advancements, and I am eagerly waiting to become a part of many such amazing things in technology that are yet to be discovered by us. I would find it wondrous if I could solve even the tiniest of problems with my software development and data science knowledge.
